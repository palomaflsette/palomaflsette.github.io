About me

My name is Paloma Sette, I'm a Control and Automation Engineering student at PUC-Rio and currently AI/ML researcher and developer at Ford Motors Company.

Driven by a passion for research and development (R&D), my focus is on creating solutions that integrate Computational intelligence, Machine Learning, and Automation systems to solve complex problems.

E-mail: paloma.sette@aluno.puc-rio.br
See more about my technological explorations ahead!

Technical area at Roche Manufacturer in Rio de Janeiro - Development and implementation of systems

My passion for science and technology wasn't born from theory; it was forged in the heat of industry, through real-world challenges and high-impact solutions.

In the industry, I had the opportunity to leave a small legacy by developing projects from concept all the way to implementation on the factory floor. Among them, I designed a "2 out of 3" redundancy system for reactors, a logic that drastically increased safety and efficiency by preventing unplanned downtime.

In another project, I implemented a Near Infrared (NIR) probe for mixing uniformity in oral solids. It was no longer necessary to stop production to collect samples and analyze them in laboratory.

After fulfilling my mission in the industry, I returned to academia with a greater thirst for knowledge. Now, I merge this valuable, hands-on experience from industry with the rigor of scientific research. My time in the industry gave me the foundation, and the university is providing me with the tools to build the future I envision. Now, I seek to build the next one at the frontier of Artificial Intelligence. The adventure continues.

GADemo

GADemo is an open educational platform designed to provide an intuitive and interactive environment for learning and experimenting with Genetic Algorithms (GAs). Created with accessibility and clarity in mind, the platform allows users to explore GA concepts through visual simulations and guided parameter manipulation — no prior coding experience required.

InsurUP

InsurUP is a specialized search platform that automates critical data discovery for reinsurance underwriters. Built in 2020 using Scrapy + AI (BERT, spaCy, scikit-learn), it performs simultaneous mining of Latin American financial information sources. With intelligent filtering by time and location, it replaced manual searches with assertive analyses, transforming financial risk assessment in the reinsurance sector.


● InsurUP Pitch Deck
● Career Talk – "Conexão LAB"
● Insurtech Program Ambassador
MUSTEM

MUSTEM is a synesthetic musical experience system for deaf individuals that combines FFT audio analysis with AI refinement to generate visual and vibrotactile patterns. Built on Arduino Mega, it processes music through Fourier Transform and CNN analysis, creating mathematically-grounded mappings that allow deaf people to "see" and "feel" music through TFT display visuals and vibratory patterns based on physical equations and psychoacoustic principles.

● Article (producing)
● First Report
● First Presentation
● Final Presentation
Raise Bot

Vision-Action Mapping system integrating OAK-D stereo camera with Pincher 4-DoF manipulator to translate 2D pixel selection into precise 3D robotic positioning. Implemented complete pipeline using coordinate transformations and inverse kinematics. This project sparked particular interest as it originated from envisioning an acoustic inspection system, with future expansion planned for piezoelectric sensor integration to analyze surface acoustic signatures for non-destructive testing.

● Final Report
● Video Demo
FinControl

FinControl is a financial control tool developed for company clients that earned recognition as the most innovative project in the "Estagiário que Ativa" program, securing 2nd place and Outstanding Intern award. The application eliminates the need for external financial tools by providing integrated asset management directly within the company ecosystem. Built with data collection and ML capabilities, it generates personalized insights and tips while enabling consensual data mining for AI-driven product development.

● Final Presentation
Lore PUC

LorePUC is a university social network app that won 3rd place at Hackeng 2018 during PUC-Rio's Engineering Week. The platform promotes continuous kindness among students through a credit-based system where helping others (tutoring, sharing information) earns points redeemable for academic event tickets and prizes. Features include peer evaluation system, real-time campus updates (library capacity, etc.), and forum-style information sharing, creating an internal social ecosystem that enhances student interaction and academic collaboration.
